Group 5 - Weekly Blog

Week 1

Group 5 came together in our Intro to Web Development class to create a web application addressing a challenge. Various project ideas were discussed, leading to our final decision: a study buddies web application for Delaware State University students.

Week 2

Our project manager, Jeremiah Whitehurst, used "Let Us Meet" to schedule meetings. User stories were developed, and the problem statement was finalized. New ideas, such as a leaderboard and restricted access features, were proposed to enhance engagement.

Week 3

Roles were finalized: Jeremiah as project manager, Hannah as designer, Javon as blogger, and Saniyah as a contributing member. The team worked on an ideation board, sign-in functionality, and database development to further advance the project.
